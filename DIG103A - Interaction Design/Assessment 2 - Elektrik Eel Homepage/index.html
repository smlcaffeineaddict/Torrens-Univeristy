Surgeon

Surgeon is a techno artist known for his innovative and hard-driving sound. He's a pioneer of the Birmingham techno scene and a respected figure in the genre. Surgeon continues to experiment and evolve his sound. His recent work reflects a focus on live improvisation and a deeper exploration of themes like the Tibetan Book of the Dead. His 2023 album "Crash Recoil" marked a return to a more direct techno sound, inspired by his live performances.

Machines Take Over: Industrial Music's exploration of Humanity and Technology in the Digital Age.

Industrial music, with its harsh sounds and dark themes, has long reflected society's anxieties about technology. From the genre's beginnings, artists explored how automation and advancements could lead to a dehumanized future controlled by machines. However, industrial music wasn't all negativity – some artists suggested a potential harmony or used the aggressive sounds for catharsis. Today, with our increasing reliance on technology, industrial music remains relevant, urging listeners to be aware of the influence of machines and fight to retain our humanity in the digital age.
