Richie Hawtin

Richie Hawtin, a British-Canadian DJ and electronic music producer, is a major figure in techno. He emerged in the early 1990s as part of Detroit techno's second wave, alongside artists like Robert Hood and Carl Craig. Hawtin became a pioneer of minimal techno, a stripped-down and hypnotic style that emphasized atmosphere and groove over complex melodies.

Robert Hood

Robert Hood, a Detroit techno legend, is revered for his pioneering role in shaping the sound of the genre. Emerging in the late 1980s alongside contemporaries like Juan Atkins and Derrick May, Hood's music is characterized by its raw, stripped-down aesthetic.

He masterfully blends elements of deep basslines, hypnotic grooves, and futuristic atmospheres, creating a sound that is both dancefloor-oriented and deeply emotional.

Surgeon

Surgeon is a techno artist known for his innovative and hard-driving sound. He's a pioneer of the Birmingham techno scene and a respected figure in the genre.

Surgeon continues to experiment and evolve his sound. His recent work reflects a focus on live improvisation and a deeper exploration of themes like the Tibetan Book of the Dead. His 2023 album "Crash Recoil" marked a return to a more direct techno sound, inspired by his live performances.

Oscar Mulero

Oscar Mulero is a Spanish techno producer and DJ who has been a major influence on the Spanish electronic scene since the early 1990s. He is also a leading exponent of underground techno, with his productions gaining widespread acclaim in the early 2000s.

Mulero is known for his dark, driving, and hypnotic techno sound. He is also the owner of two respected record labels, Warm Up Recordings and Pole Group Recordings, which have released music by some of the biggest names in techno.

Robag Wruhme

Robag Wruhme, whose real name is Gabor Schablitzki, is a German techno DJ and producer . He is well respected for his contributions to the genre, particularly for his work in minimal techno.

Wruhme's career began in the early 2000s, and he has since released music on some of the most prestigious techno labels, including Kompakt, Pampa, and his own imprint, Wighnomy Brothers. His music is known for its hypnotic grooves, intricate sound design, and subtle melodic elements.

Larry Heard AKA Mr Fingers

A true innovator, Larry Heard isn't just a founding father of deep house, but also a member of the legendary acid house trio Phuture. His soulful vocals and smooth productions like "Can You Feel It" became the blueprint for deep house, prioritizing smooth grooves and introspective melodies. Heard's influence extends beyond deep house, as Phuture's "Acid Trax" introduced the now-legendary "squelchy" acid sound that continues to be a staple of electronic music.

Dax J

A driving force in contemporary techno, Dax J injects a powerful energy into the genre. His high-octane tracks like "Paradigm Shift" and "Caravan" are techno staples played by DJs worldwide, known for their relentless energy and driving percussion. Dax J's productions are guaranteed to get crowds moving with their pulsating rhythms and electrifying atmosphere.

Luke Slater

A leading figure in British techno, Luke Slater's complex and atmospheric sound has left a lasting impact on the scene. His productions like "Alright" and "The Messenger" showcase his ability to create intricate soundscapes that are both challenging and rewarding for listeners. Slater's reputation for experimentation and releases on influential labels like Tresor Records and Rephlex solidify his place as a techno innovator.

Phuture

The trio of Phuture, featuring Larry Heard, revolutionized dance music with their iconic track "Acid Trax." This genre-defining song introduced the "squelchy" acid sound that continues to be a defining characteristic of acid house. Phuture's influence extends beyond one track, with other productions like "Slam Jack" and "Your Only Friend" remaining staples in the acid house scene. Their innovative use of the Roland TB-303 bass synthesizer forever changed the landscape of electronic music.

Joey Beltram

A pioneer of tribal house, Joey Beltram's pounding percussion and driving energy tracks like "Energy Flash" and "Set It Off" helped define the genre in the early 90s. His relentless energy and raw sound are a signature of his work, pushing the boundaries of house music with a more aggressive and percussive approach. Beltram's influence continues to be felt in techno and house music today, inspiring producers with his unapologetically raw sound.

Autechre

Autechre are electronic music innovators, constantly pushing the boundaries of techno with their complex and abstract sound. Their intricate productions like "Gantz Graf" and "Cienfuegos" are challenging and influential, characterized by a focus on experimentation and a unique sonic palette. Autechre aren't afraid to break the mold, creating music that demands repeated listens to fully appreciate the intricacies woven into their work.

Legowelt

Legowelt injects a playful yet dark aesthetic into their techno and industrial-influenced music. Tracks like "Disco Rout" showcase their quirky and energetic style, often laced with retro video game and sci-fi references. Legowelt's sound is a unique blend of influences, creating a distinct sonic identity that's both fun and dark.

Nina Kraviz

Nina Kraviz is a techno powerhouse known for her captivating stage presence and ability to weave dark and hypnotic sets. Her techno anthems like "Someway" and "Ghetto Kraviz" have become staples in the scene, showcasing her ability to create powerful and atmospheric tracks that leave a lasting impression on the dance floor. Kraviz's reputation as a DJ and producer has solidified her place as a leader in contemporary techno.

KiNK

Hailing from Bulgaria, KiNK is a leading producer in the deep house and techno scene. His music, like "Solitary Travels" and "Regarding Mountains," prioritizes melody and atmosphere, creating soundscapes that are both deep and emotionally resonant. KiNK's productions are known for their ability to transport listeners on a sonic journey, weaving intricate melodies and driving grooves that evoke a sense of introspection and emotion.

honey dijon

Honey Dijon is more than just a DJ and producer; she's a fashion icon and champion of soulful and funky house music. Her infectious energy infuses her sets and productions, like "Work" and "Hidden Track," making them irresistible dance floor anthems. Honey Dijon isn't afraid to push boundaries, incorporating elements of disco and funk into her house music, creating a sound that's both classic and fresh. Her dedication to the genre and her vibrant personality make her a true force in the underground house scene.

rebekah teasdale

A leading figure in electro and techno, Rebekah is known for her raw, energetic sound and captivating DJ sets. Her productions like "Effusion" and "DR001" showcase her ability to blend elements of techno, electro, and industrial into a powerful and driving sound. Rebekah isn't afraid to push boundaries, often incorporating distorted sounds and relentless percussion into her music, creating a unique and intense experience for listeners.
